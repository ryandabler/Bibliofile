Bibliofile is a community-driven collection of references, developed to generate a list of sources that speak to each other across all disciplines and media.

Search our list of people who have created something interesting. If a person you're looking for isn't here, you can be the one to add his or her entry.

Find information on any creative work—anything from a painting to a philosophical essay to a book review. Fill in the gaps in our database with subjects important to you.

Finally, explore a list of materials related to a subject of your choice. Great for discovering new works in a topic of interest to you or for figuring out where to start with a thesis.
